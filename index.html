Figure 1. Weekly air temperatures for the previous 12 weeks at the four observation stations.

In all charts, Max Data is the latest measurement from the database. This Week, or this whatever time period, is the date the chart was generated. Variance between the two indicates that either data for the current day is not available in the database for some reason (if This Week is later than Max Data) or someone went back in time and ran the chart scripts before the data were loaded (if This Week is earlier than Max Data).
